Villa's & rooms in our resort

-BB beach resort & SPA-



ONE-BEDROOM BEACHFRONT VILLA ESTATE

Ideal for couples seeking ultimate romance, our One-Bedroom Beachfront Villa Estate sits on a gleaming, secluded white-sand beach. The villa has its own enclosed garden, with coconut trees, bougainvilleas and tiare trees, breathtaking views of the lagoon and Mount Otemanu.



TWO-BEDROOM PREMIER BEACHFRONT VILLA ESTATE

Ideal for families or groups of friends, our Two-Bedroom Premier Beachfront Villa Estate sits on a gleaming, secluded white-sand beach. Each villa has its own enclosed garden, with coconut trees, bougainvilleas and tiare (Tahitian gardenia), a swimming pool, whirlpool, hammock, outdoor private dining options and views of the lagoon and Mount Otemanu.



TWO-BEDROOM DELUXE BEACHFRONT VILLA ESTATE

deal for families or groups of friends, our Two-Bedroom Deluxe Beachfront Villa Estate sits on a gleaming, secluded white-sand beach. Each villa has its own enclosed garden, with coconut trees, bougainvilleas and tiare (Tahitian gardenia), a swimming pool and views of the lagoon.



THREE-BEDROOM BEACHFRONT VILLA ESTATE

This one-of-a-kind villa is perfect for families and groups, with three bedrooms, four bathrooms and a private swimming pool. Located on a white-sand beach and surrounded by tropical foliage, this secluded villa offers unmatched sunset views and outdoor dining.
